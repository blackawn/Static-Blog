Css attribute writing style

January  07 , 2021
January  08 , 2021
  • css
  • css

Element attribute

display: none || block || inline-block || flex || grid ...

Css layout

display: flex
justify-content: left || center ...
align-items: left || center ...
...

display: grid
grid-template-columns: auto ...
grid-template-rows: auto ...
...

Position

position: relative || absolute || fixed ...
top: px || em || %  ...
left: px || em || % ...
bottom: px || em || %  ...
right: px || em || %  ...
z-index: 1 || -1 ...

Box model

margin: px || em || % ...
border: unit style color
padding: px || em || % ...
width: px || em || % || vw ...
height: px || em || % || vw ...

Content

font-style: italic || normal ...
font-variant: small-caps || normal ... // uncommonly used
font-weight: bold || lighter ...
font-size: px || em ...
line-height: px || em ...
font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei' || 'sans-serif' ...
...

text-align: left || center ...
text-indent: px || em ...
...

color: hex || has || rgb ...
...

Filling

background-color: hex || has || rgb ...
background-image: url("")
background-repeat: no-repeat || repeat ...
background-attachment: scroll || fixed ... // uncommonly used
background-position: x(top) || y(left) ...

background-size: cover || contain || x || y ...
background-origin: border-box || content-box ... // uncommonly used
background-clip: border-box || content-box ... // uncommonly used

//shortened
background: color, image, repeat, attachment, position / size, origin, clip
